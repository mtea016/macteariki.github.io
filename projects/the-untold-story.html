The untold story of teen suicide in the North

A Herald long-form investigation about a cluster of teen suicides in Northland in 2012. This was the first in a series of stories as part of a month long campaign on youth suicide that ran in June-July 2017.

I was the lead designer, developer and UX working in a multidisciplinary team including Olivia Carville (investigative journalist) and Mike Scott (visual journalist). I used HTML, Sass, Javascript with the help of Gulp to design and build the presentation.
