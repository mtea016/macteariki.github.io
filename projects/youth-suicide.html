The untold story of teen suicide in the North

A Herald long-form investigation about a cluster of teen suicides in Northland in the year 2012. This was the first in a series of stories as part of a week long campaign on youth suicide that ran in June-July 2017. HTML, Sass and Javascript were used to design and build the presentation. I was also involved in content curation.
