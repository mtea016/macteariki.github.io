Age of Reality: A journey through NZ with David Fisher

David Fisher and videographer Mark Mitchell spent almost a month driving round New Zealand in a campervan. Their mission: to find out what it means to be a New Zealander. Age of Reality was the final part of a month long Herald series and maps the exact route they took, starting from Bluff all the way up to Cape Reinga.

This uses a map element and a route tracker that is activated as the story is scrolled. HTML and Sass were used to design and build the project. We used the Mapbox API to render the map and Javascript to draw the long and winding path from the bottom of NZ to the very top.
