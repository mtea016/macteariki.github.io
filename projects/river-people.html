The River People : Death and Life on the Kaituna River

The River People is a Herald feature presentation that follows the story of Louise Jull, one of the country's most renowned whitewater kayakers who went under in the rapids of the Kaituna River and never came up.

I was responsible for the design and build and worked in collaboration with Dana Johanssen (journalist) and Nick Reed (video and photography). The project uses HTML, Sass, Javascriptand Gulp.
